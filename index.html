Alfaro Law is a private law firm that services all areas of Sydney and regional New South Wales. At Alfaro Law, we are dedicated, professional and friendly team, and pride ourselves on delivering exceptional legal services and results to our diverse client base.

We offer a multi-practice service and are able to assist our clients with the majority of their legal issues. Clients benefit from our extensive knowledge of the law, vast network of professional and legal advisers as well as our unmatched client service experience.

At Alfaro Law, we understand the complex nature of the law and legal transactions and strive to simplify the process for our clients. Through communication, knowledge and transparency we help our clients understand where they want to be and how they can get there. Whether it is a straightforward legal transaction or complex legal dispute, the law is constantly changing and with clients ranging from individuals to corporations we ensure that our legal solutions are up-to-date and tailored to our clients’ individual needs.
